Curious Minds

How do I support sustainable fashion?

Buy less, choose well, and make it last! Support ethical brands, shop secondhand, and invest in quality pieces.

How can I reduce textile waste?

Donate, swap, or upcycle your used clothing, and always consider repurposing items before discarding them.

What is textile recycling?

Textile recycling reclaims used fabrics, repurposing them into new products or breaking them down to create new textiles.

Do eco-friendly fabrics exist?

Yes! Organic cotton, linen, hemp, bamboo, and Tencel are all sustainable alternatives to fast fashion fabrics.
